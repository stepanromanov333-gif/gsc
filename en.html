Discover the world of GSC


Georgian Sailing Club (GSC) is a modern yacht club located on the picturesque shore of the Tbilisi Sea, just 10 km from the centre of Tbilisi — where the energy of urban life meets the freedom of sailing.


GSC — more than just a yacht club.


It is a lifestyle. A hub for active, modern and successful people. A space where the city stays on the shore while you discover a new level of energy and inspiration.

The club’s mission is to develop sailing, making it accessible, stylish and inspiring.
We are building a modern sailing culture in Tbilisi by combining training, sport, leisure and a sense of unity. All this takes place in a unique waterfront location where comfort meets excitement and the aesthetics of movement.

We provide sailing instruction and conduct training sessions on modern yachts. We organise boat trips, corporate and private outings on the water, as well as regattas of various levels — from amateur races to professional competitions.


The GSC is home to the Skipper School and the Children’s Sailing School.
