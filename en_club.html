Georgian Sailing Club

The Georgian Sailing Club organises and hosts regular club regattas, children’s and youth competitions, as well as family mini‑regattas.
The club provides services for organising and conducting training camps, crew preparation and technical support ahead of participation in national and international regattas — including series events and the RS21 World Championship.
Georgian Sailing Club is a place where emotions, friendship and a love for the wind and sail are born.
