Georgian Sailing Club Fleet

Heart of the Club

Modern, stylish, and meticulously curated — the GSC fleet is designed to let everyone experience the true freedom of sailing.
Our yachts stand out for their lightness of movement, reliability, and safety, delivering pure joy of gliding across the water — whether you’re a beginner, a seasoned professional, or a regatta participant.
    • RS21 — The energy of speed and team synergy.
    The flagship of our fleet and an official international racing class.
    A modern sports yacht combining high performance, ease of handling, and absolute safety even in strong winds.
    Every outing on the RS21 inspires, energizes, and delivers unforgettable experiences.

    Elegant design, thoughtful ergonomics, and perfect balance make these yachts a symbol of progressive sailing. Regardless of your experience level, our professional club instructors will help you take your first steps under sail or hone your racing skills.

    • Hull length: 6.8 meters
    • Crew capacity: 3–4 sailors

    • Built for training, regattas, and team‑building races

    Book RS21
    • RS QUEST — Comfort and confidence under sail.
    A versatile boat for sailing trips, training, and practice sessions — equally suitable for both beginners and experienced yachtsmen.
    It is an excellent choice for family weekends or lessons with an instructor.

    Experience the harmony of wind, sun, and a snow‑white sail — surrounded by your family or friends.


    • Capacity: 2–4 people
    • Suitable for leisurely sailing trips and training

    • Lightness, stability, and safety

    Book RS Quest

    • RS FEVA — Youth, energy, movement.


    A modern two‑person yacht designed for young and passionate sailors.

    This lightweight planing dinghy is easy to handle — suitable for both beginners and experienced athletes. The crew can consist of two teenagers, an adult with a child, or a single sailor.

    The RS Feva class is internationally recognized: winner of «Dinghy of the Year» in the USA, laureate of «Coup de Coeurs» in France, endorsed by the RYA, and holding ISAF status. Since 2003, world, European, and national championships have been held.


    • A popular international youth class

    • Ideal for training, practice sessions, and family crews

    Book RS Feva

    • ILCA (LASER) — Purity of line and strength of character.

    An Olympic classic of single‑handed sailing.


    The ILCA allows you to gain a deeper understanding of boat handling through sail control, managing heel, and precise adjustment of all parameters. The skills acquired build confidence and become the foundation for mastering crewed yachts.


    A perfect tool for those seeking harmony between technique, speed, and the feeling of freedom under sail.


    • An individual sailing class

    • Versatile for sports training and international‑level competitions

    Book ILCA

    OPTIMIST — A classic of children’s sailing sport.

    A legendary small boat where millions of children around the world first experienced what it means to harness the wind.


    Optimist — the first step into the world of competitive sailing, a symbol of independence and confidence on the water.


    • For children aged 6–14 years

    • Safe, stable, and reliable

    Book Optimist

    CONRAD — Balance, knowledge, reboot.

    The keel yacht Conrad is designed for those who wish to spend a day on the water — whether for yachting lessons or a relaxing sail.

    On board, you can take a practical session with an instructor, learn how the boat is structured, and try your hand at sail handling.

    You can also enjoy a leisurely sail, take a swim, and capture photos at sunset.


    • Spacious and safe hull

    • Introduction to the yacht’s structure and systems

    • Scenic cruises with a captain

    Book Conrad
